What is Trézór Suite?

How to Set Up Trézór Suite

Get started with your Trézór Wallet Setup in minutes:

  1. Go to Trézór.io/start to download Trézór Suite.
  2. Connect your Trézór Hardware using a USB cable.
  3. Complete the Totone Install and firmware setup.
  4. Securely back up your recovery seed offline.
  5. Create a PIN for your Trézór Wallet.
  6. Select cryptocurrencies to manage.
